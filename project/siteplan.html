Site Name

Motorcycle Club City

I chose this name because it represents a local club dedicated to motorcycle enthusiasts, giving a sense of community and belonging. It’s easy to remember and reflects the identity of the city.

Site Purpose

The site’s purpose is to provide information about the motorcycle club, including its history, events, photos, and how to join. It serves as a platform to keep members informed and attract new people interested in joining the club.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming events of the club?
  • How can I join the club and participate in the activities?

Color Schema

Color 1: #1E1E1E (background and main headings)

Color 2: #FF5733 (accent color for buttons and links)

Typography

Font 1: Arial, for the body text.

Font 2: Montserrat, for headings and titles.

Mobile Wireframe

A simple design where the header is visible at the top, followed by a collapsible navigation menu, a featured image of the bike in the main section, and a contact form at the footer.

Desktop Wireframe

The layout includes a fixed navigation bar at the top, a hero image spanning the header, a central body with a list of events, and a photo gallery in a grid, with a contact form and map in the footer.
